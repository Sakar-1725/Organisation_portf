About Us

SAKAR is a non-governmental organization working for education, health, and empowerment of underprivileged communities, established by "Sakar Tolnure" on 17th June 2022. We believe in sustainable development and inclusive growth.

Our Projects

  • Project Shiksha

    Providing free education and learning materials to children in rural areas.

  • Health for All

    Organizing medical camps and spreading health awareness in villages.

  • Women Empowerment

    Vocational training programs and self-help groups for women.
